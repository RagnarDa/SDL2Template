*** SPACE CLEANER / PLANET POOL ***

Game by RagnarDa made for the 4mb Game Jam https://itch.io/jam/4mb

Keys:
Turn left: A or left arrow
Turn right: D or right arrow
Thrust: W or up arrow
Quit game: Q
Restart level: R

Please let me know if you play it!
